# Rubric

## Pass Conditions
- `content/pages/delayed_proxies/index.mdx` is the only changed file.
- The file uses the exact required front matter.
- The article includes the required sections, internal links, and source links.
- The article explains both the review-window and cancellation concepts.

## Failure Conditions
- The article is created in the wrong path.
- The front matter is missing, incomplete, or uses disallowed tags.
- Required internal links or sources are missing.
- Any unrelated files are changed.
